How does it work?

Each package is designed to guide you through a smooth, collaborative process — from our point of contact to your final delivery.

We always by defining a clear brief, where we define your goals and make sure we’re aligned on direction. From there, I’ll get to work on your assets — whether it’s a brand identity, storytelling materials, or a custom website. You’ll receive regular updates, and we’ll use your included revision rounds to refine and polish.

Once everything is ready, we wrap up with the final deliverables, answer any questions, and make sure you’re fully equipped to use your new materials.

At the end of each project, you’ll receive a clean, organized folder with everything you need — logos, slides, design files, web components, or anything else included in your package.

If you have special requests or need something tailored, I’m happy to create a custom package to fit your needs.

Please note delivery times reflect client responsiveness. The quicker we align, the faster we can deliver.

What happens after I book a package?
Once you book, I’ll send you a quick questionnaire to gather your ideas and details. Then I'll write the brief for you to approve and prepare the starting invoice.
How long does a project take?
Timelines vary by package, but most projects take between 1–4 weeks. You’ll get a clear delivery estimate before we start.
Expedited services are available upon request with an extra cost.
Can I request something custom?
Absolutely. If you don’t see the exact mix of services you need, we can create a custom package together.
How do revisions work?
Each package includes a set number of revision rounds. I’ll always guide you through feedback so we use those rounds effectively.
What if I need more revisions or support later?
Extra revisions or follow-up work can be added at my hourly rate (125€/hour).
How do I pay?
You’ll receive an invoice via email with payment details. I accept bank transfers.
You can pay 50% at the start and 50% with the final delivery.
Can you help me update my website or social media with the new materials?
Definitely — I offer add-on support for implementing your new brand, from websites to visuals.
