FAQ

We start with an introductory phone call and then we set a clear brief, where we define your goals and make sure we’re on the same page. Once we define the deliverables and the timeline, I will create the starting invoice and once payment is completed I will get to work.

From there, I’ll focus on your project and you’ll receive regular updates. I can work independently or join your team's meetings to ensure we deliver the best value to our users.

Once everything is ready, we wrap up with the final deliverables, answer any questions, and make sure you’re fully equipped to use your new materials.

If you have special requests or need something tailored, I’m happy to create a custom package to fit your needs. You can read more details about my design process here.

Please note delivery times reflect client responsiveness. The quicker we align, the faster we can deliver.

How much do projects cost?
My daily rate is 700€. The final price is calculated based on how much time we estimate the project will take.
Extra revisions or follow-up work can be added at my hourly rate (100€/hour).
What happens after I book a package?
Once you book, I’ll send you a quick questionnaire to gather your ideas and details. Then we'll have an introductory call and I'll write the brief for you to approve and prepare the starting invoice.
How long does a project take?
Timelines vary by package, but most projects take between 1–4 weeks. You’ll get a clear delivery estimate before we start.
Expedited services are available upon request with an extra cost.
Can I request something custom?
Absolutely. If you don’t see the exact mix of services you need, we can create a custom package together.
What if I need more revisions or support later?
Extra revisions or follow-up work can be added at my hourly rate (100€/hour).
How do I pay?
You’ll receive an invoice via email with payment details. I accept bank transfers.
You can pay 50% at the start and 50% with the final delivery.
